From Chairman's Pen

In these fast paced economy Youths are the engine room of societies. They are the drivers of any developmental trend and activity in society and major determinants of the extent of growth and development in any given society. Youths are usually very energetic and are always willing to go the extra mile if need be to achieve what they believe in and hold on to. Therefore, anything targeted at this group of persons must be worthwhile and directed at helping them fulfill their purpose as would-be leaders. As youths get prepared for leadership roles; it is pertinent to acquire moral standards and values that will mold them into personalities ready to lead for the progress of the society Everyone wants to be wanted and needed; feelings of belonging are crucial for a person’s self-esteem. Unfortunately, people are not born with these skills, although some individuals learn them more readily than others. Learning how to get along with others is a process that begins at a very early stage and continues throughout life. In this process of learning the books play a very significant role. It is said that “When you open a book, you open a new world”. For the majority of people, books are part of their everyday life.  A book is like a best friend who will never walk away from you.Books are packed with knowledge, insights into a happy life, life lessons, love, fear, prayer and helpful advice. My compilation of books was necessitated due to the conundrum of opinions the young minds are subjected to in these recent days. The positive application of this exhaustive collation is that it inspires the young minds to inculcate the social values and life lessons in their personality which will help them to survive in this technology packed society. Human interaction in the workplace involves team production, with workers playing off of each other’s strengths and adapting flexibly to changing circumstances. Such non-routine interaction is at the heart of the human advantage over machines. Social skills are a vital part of a Student’s growth and development. While we are increasingly connected on the digital front, we are also becoming less connected in everyday life. More children are growing up in this fast-paced and frenetic environment. As they move on to adulthood and the job market, learning appropriate methods of social interaction at an early age will be even more important. My books brilliantly efforts that point that if you make use of these distinct thoughts, you can transform your life.
