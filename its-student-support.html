Student Support

I.T.S Institutions

I.T.S – The Education Group, believes in keeping its students abreast with the latest developments, happenings and opportunities for their better career prospects through active career counseling cells at its various institutes. I.T.S Group believes in the philosophy that healthy mind rests in healthy body and to ensure this health centers where routine health checkups of students are done periodically are created at all the campuses.

The dedicated team of faculty members and professional counselors help aspiring students to identify career options which are suitable to their talents and interests. This team also helps students in understanding their strengths, identify an aligned career options and to work on weaknesses to improve upon. Regular workshops on Personality grooming, interviewing skills and technical subjects prepares students ready to serve in industry. These efforts have immensely helped students in their recruitment and career growth.

For counseling sessions, students may approach or visit at following office during 09.00 AM to 05.30 PM:

I.T.S – The Education Group understands and recognizes the importance of student-mentor relationship as it plays crucial role developing confidence among students with its imprints on student’s life. This also helps in building up an emotional connect and long term relationship between students, faculty and institute, encourages students to share, discuss and resolved their academic or personal issues. Institutes, under the umbrella of I.T.S Group have adopted and formalized Mentor-Mentee Concept (MMC) to help mentees in understanding them, identifying and formulating plans for excelling in academics and their career options by exploiting their strengths and working on weaknesses to improve upon.

Objective of Mentor-Mentee concept is to support students in their academic and other issues resolved better, to make them a confident and at the same time provide them individualized attention and care so that they do not left on their own. The Mentor-Mentee Concept (MMC) exists to aid newly admitted students familiarize, understand and adopt a new academic & learning environment by removing the issues of getting acquaintance with new environment during initial phase arising out of shyness, fears by communicating, expectations and optimism.
