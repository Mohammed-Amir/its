Scholarships for Courses @ I.T.S Ghaziabad 

I.T.S Group has always been encouraging, promoting and creating avenues for a healthy competitive environment among students to excel in their academics. To recognize and appreciate the efforts of the students, institute offers scholarships to the meritorious students of all the courses. Selected schemes are also offered to aspiring students seeking admission to various courses at the institutes.

Academic Performance Awards
Merit Award

For II year students based on their performance in I year End semester examination. It is awarded to top three meritorious students of each batch at the end of each year. First topper gets a scholarship of Rs. 15,000/- , Second topper gets a scholarship of Rs. 10,000/- and third topper gets a scholarship of Rs. 5,000/-.


Performance Improvement Award

It is awarded to top 10 students who show maximum improvement in terms of increase in percentage from the previous semester exams to the next semester exams of I and II year respectively.

SCHOLARSHIPS AND AWARDS

To encourage academic Performance and to reward excellence in academics, I.T.S has instituted a system of awarding scholarships to deserving Students.

Scholarships are to be awarded to the Students at the time of admission. These scholarships, in the form of discount in fees are offered to students who have secured a certain percentage in class XII (percentage of all five subjects, excluding any additional subject).

The details are enumerated as below:

  • Topper’s Scholarship Scheme
  • Merit Scholarship Scheme
  • Siblings Scholarship Scheme
  • Scholarship under Sports Category
  • Scholarship for Jammu & Kashmir and North East Region
  • Scholarship under Defence Category
